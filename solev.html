Synopsis The story is set in a modern world where a mysterious “gate” appears, connecting the human world with a monster-filled dungeon. Humans with special abilities, called “Hunters,” fight in this dungeon to earn rewards and keep the monsters out. Sung Jinwoo, the main protagonist, is known as the “Weakest Hunter in All of Humanity” due to his minimal strength. After almost dying in a dangerous dungeon, Jinwoo gains a unique ability from the “System,” a game-like interface that allows him to level up like in an RPG. With this new power, Jinwoo transforms from the weakest to one of the strongest Hunters, unraveling the mysteries behind the System, the gates, and the origins of his powers while facing ever-greater threats.
