Synopsis Magic Emperor tells the story of Zhuo Yifan, a Demon Emperor (or Magic Emperor) who has extraordinary powers thanks to the Book of Nine Secrets, a highly coveted ancient book. Because of his powers, he becomes the target of martial arts masters and is eventually betrayed and killed by his trusted disciple. However, his soul was reborn in the body of a weak noble family servant named Zhuo Fan. Bound by demonic magic and the boy's memories, Zhuo Fan must serve the young lady of the Luo Family while plotting his resurrection. With his wits, strategy, and past experience, he seeks to lift this nearly ruined family back to the pinnacle of power on the continent, while facing political intrigue, powerful enemies, and the secrets of his past.
